✦ Our Story

Born in a
Lagos Kitchen.

Mojoyin started as Mama's Sunday ritual — the whole neighbourhood knew when her Ayamase was on the fire. The aroma of bleached palm oil, iru, and green pepper would drift through Isale Eko before 9am.

What began as a gift to friends became a movement. Today, every jar and every pot we send out carries the same love, the same Yoruba pride, and the same stubborn refusal to cut corners.

🌿 100% Natural No preservatives, no shortcuts. Just ingredients you can pronounce.
🏺 Handcrafted Every batch made fresh, the traditional Yoruba way.
🚚 Fast Delivery Same-day across Lagos, next-day nationwide.
